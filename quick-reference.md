| Attribute | The Condition | Not This |
|-----------|--------------|---------|
| **Calm** | We removed the chaos before the client felt it. | Passive. Slow. "No worries!" without solving anything. |
| **Straight** | We say what happened. We give people what they need to decide. | Corporate deflection. Hedge-and-soften. |

## Words We Never Use

| Always Wrong | Why |
|-------------|-----|
| fur baby, doggo, pupper, floof | Baby talk. Puts us in the squeaky toy aisle. |
| pawsome, pawsitively, pawfect | Puns aren't our register. |
| we're passionate about / committed to / strive to | Hedging. Commit or don't. |
| amazing team / absolutely fabulous | Adjectives pretending to be evidence. |
| per our policy / at this time | Call center language. We are not a call center. |
| delve, tapestry, unleash, game-changer | AI tells. Break trust that a human wrote this. |

## Before You Write

Ask yourself: *What do I want the reader to be able to do after reading this?*

1. Empower them
2. Respect them as an intelligent adult
3. Educate them — what they need, not what you want credit for
4. Guide them to a next step
5. Tell the truth

---

## Five Rules Worth Memorizing

1. **Lead with the fact.** Open with what they came for. Context follows.
2. **15 words or fewer** for core sentences. Explanation follows the anchor.
3. **Active voice.** Every passive construction is a rewrite trigger.
4. **No superlatives without proof.** "We'll call within 2 hours" — not "we aim to respond promptly."
5. **Stakes up = formality up.** Never the reverse.

---

## Voice Check — Five Questions

1. Would Dana actually say this?
2. Could any other Austin dog groomer have written this? *(If yes — rewrite.)*
3. Does every word carry information?
4. Warm without gushy? Expert without clinical?
5. If there's humor — did the situation give it to us, or did we force it?

## When Something Goes Wrong

One pattern. Works for every crisis:

**Name the specific thing → take responsibility or explain clearly → give a concrete next step → move it offline.**

Do not perform empathy. Do the thing empathy would lead to.
